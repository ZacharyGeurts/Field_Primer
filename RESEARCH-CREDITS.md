| Researcher / tradition | Contribution | Where it appears |
|------------------------|--------------|------------------|
| **James Clerk Maxwell** | Field formulation of electromagnetism; coupled continuous state | Phi / Flow fabric metaphor, wave PDE steps |
| **Rudolf Clausius · Ludwig Boltzmann** | Entropy as irreversibility in thermodynamic systems | Thermo channel, entropy floor, Second Law framing |
| **Rolf Landauer** | Minimum energy of irreversible bit erasure: E_min = k_B T ln 2 | `ThermoAccountant`, Chapter 4 |
| **Claude Shannon** | Information entropy H = −Σ pᵢ log₂ pᵢ | NEXUS entropy oracle, Chapter 4 |

| **Courant · Friedrichs · Lewy (CFL)** | Stability condition for explicit time-stepping of PDEs | Host `waveCFL` / `thermoCFL` guards, Ch 3 |
| **John von Neumann** | Stored-program computing architecture | Field Die guest map — digital state in address space |
| **Alan Turing** | Computability and the limits of mechanized inference | Honesty labels — what machines can and cannot claim |

We cite theory; we do not claim laboratory reproduction of every equation on silicon.
